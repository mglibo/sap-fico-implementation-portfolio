Business Scenario

NovaTech Germany needed a domestic German supplier for laboratory chemicals. The vendor had to be available for company code NT01 and purchasing organization NT01 so it can later support purchasing, invoice posting and payment processing.

Support Thinking

Support & Troubleshooting Cases

Example SAP FI/CO support scenarios used to show analysis approach and transaction awareness.

Vendor invoice not selected in F110

Scenario

A due vendor invoice does not appear in the automatic payment proposal.

Analysis approach

Check payment block, baseline date, payment terms, vendor payment method, house bank setup, proposal log, and open item status.

SAP areas/transactions

AP, Automatic Payment Program, FB03, FBL1N, FK03/XK03, F110, FBZP.

What this demonstrates

Understanding of payment run logic, master data dependency, and structured support triage.

Posting period closed during month-end

Scenario

A finance user cannot post a document because the target accounting period is closed.

Analysis approach

Validate company code, posting date, document type, account type, period variant, and month-end close calendar before proposing a change.

SAP areas/transactions

FI Global Settings, GL/AP/AR postings, OB52, FB50, FB60, FB70.

What this demonstrates

Month-end support awareness, control mindset, and change-process discipline.

Tax line not generated correctly

Scenario

An invoice posts without the expected tax line or with an unexpected tax amount.

Analysis approach

Review tax code, country procedure, account tax category, vendor/customer tax classification, calculation base, and posting simulation.

SAP areas/transactions

Tax on Sales/Purchases, GL account master, FTXP, OB40, FS00, FB60, FB70.

What this demonstrates

Ability to connect tax configuration, master data, and transactional posting behavior.

GR/IR mismatch

Scenario

Goods receipt and invoice receipt values do not clear as expected for a purchase order.

Analysis approach

Compare PO history, GR quantity/value, invoice quantity/value, account assignment, price variance, and clearing status.

SAP areas/transactions

MM-FI integration, GR/IR clearing, ME23N, MB03/MIGO, MIR4, FBL3N, MR11.

What this demonstrates

Cross-module thinking between procurement, inventory receipt, invoice verification, and FI clearing.

Parked document not posted

Scenario

A parked FI document remains unposted and blocks expected reporting or payment processing.

Analysis approach

Check document completeness, workflow/approval status, missing required fields, authorization messages, and posting period availability.

SAP areas/transactions

FI document parking, workflow support, FV60, FBV0, FBV3, SU53, OB52.

What this demonstrates

Support approach for user process issues, authorizations, validation rules, and finance document lifecycle.

Scenario Background & Business Case

Current State (AS-IS)
  • Legacy ERP system (15+ years old) with limited reporting capabilities
  • Fragmented financial data across 3 subsidiaries with manual consolidation
  • No real-time cost controlling or profitability analysis
  • Manual bank reconciliation processes consuming 40+ hours/month
  • Separate chart of accounts per subsidiary — no standardization
Future State (TO-BE)
  • SAP ECC 6.0 with unified Chart of Accounts across all entities
  • Financial reporting using SAP standard reports and custom queries
  • Automated bank statement processing and reconciliation
  • Integrated cost controlling with drill-down profitability analysis
  • Centralized shared services for AP/AR processing
